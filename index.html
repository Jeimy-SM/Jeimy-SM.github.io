South Korea Facts, Culture and Language

FACTS:

Location: Eastern Asia, southern half of the Korean Peninsula bordering the East Sea and the Yellow Sea
Capital: Seoul
National anthem: Aegukga
Nationality: Korean
Climate: temperate, with rainfall heavier in summer than winter
Currency: South Korean ‘won’
Government: Republic
Language: Korean
Alphabet: Hangul

CULTURE:

Influenced by chinese and japanese cultures.
Showing respect to the elders and family.
Koreans bow when meeting people.

KPOP:

Kpop is short for Korean Pop.
In kpop there a different types of music styles.

LANGUAGE(BASIC WORDS):

Hello/Goodbye – 안녕하세요 – An-nyeong-ha-se-yo
Nice to meet you – 반갑습니다 – Ban-gap-sum-ni-da
Thank you – 감사합니다 – Kam-sa-ham-ni-da
Excuse me/just a moment – 잠시만요 – Jam-shi-man-yo
